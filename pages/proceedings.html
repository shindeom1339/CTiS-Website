Proceedings

The third conference on Computational Thinking in Schools (CTiS2021) was held virtually at School of Scholars, Nagpur from 29th September to 2nd October 2021 and saw participation of more than 1100 delegates which included teachers, Principals, educators and organisations working in the field of education from across 15 countries and 19 states in India.

CTiS2021 featured Keynote Speakers Hal Abelson, MIT, USA, Manish Jain, IIT Gandhinagar, Patricia Ordóñez, University of Puerto Rico Río Piedras, Wolfgang Slany, TU Graz, Austria, Supriya Dey, Vision Empower, Manohar Swaminathan, Microsoft Research, Bengaluru

CTiS2021 also had teacher presentations across various thematic areas, CT and Coding, CT for professional development of teachers, CT implementation and CT in Mathematics Education.

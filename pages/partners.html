The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, fostering knowledge, collaboration and innovation. ACM unities professionals, researchers, educators, and students to inspire dialogue and ideas that address the field's challenges. ACM provides career development, professional networking, and lifelong learning.

ACM Nagpur Professional Chapter has been working towards the above goals by executing various local events such as technical talks, workshops, and conferences. It carries out these activities in association with ACM India, various local organizations and, academic and professional institutes. Some of the key recent events executed by this chapter include talks by speakers from ACM Eminent Speaker Program, ACM India Annual Day 2018, COMPUTE 2020, ACM-W India Celebration of Women in Computing 2020, and CSPathshala workshops for schools.
